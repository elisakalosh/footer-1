ABOUT US

ZASHA ACTIONEERS is a licensed actioneering firm with vast experience and a proven track record of conducting successful actions across a diverse range of industries.Our team combines deep knowledge of auction dynamics with exceptional communication and crowd-engagement skills to create an electrifying atmosphere that maximizes bidding and drives exceptional results.

We are registered and active member of NAKA (National Association of Kenya Auctioneers)

We adhere to high ethical standards and ensure that auctions are conducted fairly and transparently.The zasha Auctioneers team is well-versed in the legal and regulatory requirements related to auctions.

At Zasha Auctioneers we value excellent customer service.We interact with both buyers and sellers professionally, addressing questions and concerns to create a positive auction experience.
